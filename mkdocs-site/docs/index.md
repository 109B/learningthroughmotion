## What Was Built

A complete **SEND-focused educational website** for Learning Through Motion, featuring:

- **Modern Next.js 16** architecture with App Router
- **Accessibility-first design** (WCAG AAA compliance)
- **3 programme detail pages** with rich content
- **4 interactive educational games** for SEND learners
- **Dynamic image carousels** across the site
- **Testimonials system** with modal expansion
- **Contact/enquiry system** with multiple CTAs
- **Community session booking** information pages

## The Client

**Learning Through Motion** delivers cross-curricular learning through sport and movement for children with SEND (Special Educational Needs and Disabilities) across Greater Manchester and Lancashire.

### Three Core Programmes

1. **Maths Through Sport** - Curriculum-aligned maths through physical activities
2. **Sensory Redevelopment** - Movement-based sensory integration sessions
3. **The Next Chapter** - Year 7 transition mentoring programme
